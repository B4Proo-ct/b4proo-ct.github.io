## 🌟 Description

P2P_File_Transfer is a secure and fast file-sharing system. Built using Python and Django, this application allows you to share files directly between devices without hassle. Whether you have a good internet connection or not, our backup Upload & Share mode ensures your files go through every time.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

Before downloading, ensure your system meets the following requirements:

- **Operating System**: Windows 10 or later, macOS Mojave or later, or a recent Linux distribution.
- **Python Version**: Python 3.7 or later installed on your machine.
- **Web Browser**: A modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.

## 🔄 How to Use P2P_File_Transfer

1. **Select Files**: Click the "Select Files" button to choose the files you want to share.
2. **Choose Transfer Mode**: Decide between direct peer-to-peer transfer or the backup Upload & Share mode.
3. **Share**: Click "Share" to start the process. Receive a unique link for your shared files, which you can send to others.
